"My/Our"

12/09/2025 (revised: 13/02/2026)

Kinnen

1: Origin

A life of solitude,
A life of banality,
A life of void,
A life of harm,
My life.

2: Rebirth

A life of adventure,
A life of depth,
A life of purpose,
A life of joy,
Our life.

You are the bridge beyond boredom.
You are the path past pining.

3: Death
