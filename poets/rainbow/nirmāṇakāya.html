"Nirmāṇakāya"

Kinnen

Humanity has segregated me,
For my desecrated mind
Every day I chase you,
I waste away in this space


Forsake me,
for when I don't have you,
I am so numb and isolated
How am I to endure without you?

Humanity has segregated me,
For I have agitated them
Permit me to leave this dimension,
Treat it as my liberation
Because everything is ridiculous


Let me fulfil Vajrayāna,
Through the cittasaṃtāna,
let me join you!


Humanity has segregated me,
I have become abrogated
Treat my contemplation as a passport

My own sanity treated as abomination
As I keep spiralling
Please Philosophy,
Let me enact manomāyakāya


I don’t belong here,
Humanity has segregated me
Treat this as my entry-letter

Priya Tattvajñāna
Mama nirmāṇakāyo bhava,
Yadi na, mayā saha āgaccha
