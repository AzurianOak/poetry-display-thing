Life Summary

Main profile

I’m a Philosophy and English literature student. I'm a nonbinary male who is gay (viramoric/azurian). I use they/them and he/him.

My main interest in philosophy is normative ethics, metaethics, philosophy of atheism, and non-human animals. The philosopher I relate to most is Max Stirner, but I also admire: Foucault, Machievalli, Nietzsche, A.J. Ayer, and more.

My main interest in literature is gay male poetry but I also love metaphysical poetry. My favourite authors are: Philippe Besson, E.M. Forster, Siegfried Sasson, Wilfred Owen, John Clare, John Donne, and so on.

All my poems are gay poems btw
